Laws Online/Catalogue

View and/or order Alberta legislation (acts, regulations, codes) and legislative publications by searching the catalogue below. See Purchasing help for information about project options.

Recommended browsers include Google Chrome, Firefox or Microsoft Edge. Please note, content may display differently depending on the device used (mobile, computer, tablet etc.).

Unless otherwise indicated:

Acts current to 2025-12-10
Regulations current to 2025-12-14

Due to the Canada Post service interruption, when placing an order for print materials, please indicate your delivery preference when ordering on-line. Options are:

  • In-person pick-up at our Edmonton office – Suite 700, 10611 98 Avenue.
  • Use a courier of your choice – please provide your courier name and your account number.
  • Authorize Alberta King’s Printer to send the order out by courier for an additional $10.50 fee.
  • Hold order until the strike is over – your item(s) will be sent out once regular postal service resumes.

If your order value exceeds $500 no extra charge for shipping will be required. There is no impact to orders regularly sent out by Government Courier.
