BC Laws Buzz

BC Laws Modernization

The new Statutes Act and updated Regulations Act have received Royal Assent. Both Acts will modernize the BC Laws website. Read more and keep track of the progress here.

Commercial Liens Act

The Commercial Liens Act came into force on June 30, 2025 and has been added to BC Laws.

New Acts - Spring 2025

The Economic Stabilization (Tariff Response) Act, the Eligibility to Hold Public Office Act, the Infrastructure Projects Act, and the Perinatal and Postnatal Mental Health Strategy Act are now on BC Laws.
