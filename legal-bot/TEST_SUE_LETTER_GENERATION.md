## Tips for Your Specific Case

### Property Damage Cases Usually Require:

1. **Documentation:**
   - Before and after photos
   - Repair estimates (get 2-3 quotes)
   - Property survey or boundary documentation
   - Communication records with neighbor

2. **Evidence:**
   - Witness testimony
   - Expert testimony (if needed)
   - Police reports (if applicable)
   - Insurance documentation

3. **Damages Calculation:**
   - Cost to repair or replace
   - Loss of property value
   - Temporary housing costs (if applicable)
   - Professional assessment fees

### Common Property Damage Claims:

- **Fence damage**: $500 - $5,000
- **Landscaping**: $1,000 - $10,000
- **Structural damage**: $5,000 - $50,000+
- **Water damage**: $2,000 - $20,000+

## Cost Considerations

### Small Claims Court (Recommended for amounts under $10,000):
- **Pros**: Faster, cheaper, no attorney required
- **Cons**: Lower damage limits, limited appeals
- **Cost**: $30-$100 filing fee
- **Time**: 30-90 days

### Superior/District Court (For larger amounts):
- **Pros**: No damage limit, full legal process
- **Cons**: Expensive, time-consuming, attorney recommended
- **Cost**: $200-$500 filing fee + attorney fees
- **Time**: 6-24 months
