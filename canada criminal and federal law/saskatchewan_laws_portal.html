Visit the Legislative Building

Learn about Saskatchewan's history with a free tour in English or French. Our fun and interesting guided visit of the Saskatchewan Legislative Building offers a glimpse of the Assembly's work, as well as a history of the building's construction.
 
Tours are available seven days a week, from 9:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.
 
Holiday tour hours:
  • Dec. 24: 9:00-11:00 a.m. and 1:00-2:00 p.m.
  • Dec. 25 and 26: Closed
  • Dec. 27 to 30: 9:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.
  • Dec. 31: 9:00-11:00 a.m. and 1:00-2:00 p.m.
  • Jan. 1: Closed
  • Jan. 2: 9:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.

French tours are offered at 10:00 a.m. and 3:00 p.m., Monday to Friday.
